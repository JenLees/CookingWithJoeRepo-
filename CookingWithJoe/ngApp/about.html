About Cooking With Joe

Hi, I'm Jenny and I'm a Trader-Joe's-aholic. A couple of decades ago I had my frist Trader Joe's experience as a young woman at my local location in Fair Oaks, CA. I don't remember my first product but what got me hooked was a spicy peanut snack mix. Oh how I miss that spicy peanut snack mix.

I moved from Caliornia to Seattle, WA in 1999 and to my disappointment, there wasn't a Trader Joe's store near me. But lucky for me one opened a few months after my move. I started doing most of my weekly grocery shopping there and found I was eating healthier, spending less money, and no longer dreaded the weekly drudgery of shopping for food.

Fast forward a decade. I had gotten married and my husband Shane was also a big fan of Trader Joe's. He started coming up with great meals made from what we purchased there. A few years ago he suggested we start a blog of recipes and products we love. Life of course got in the way but now here it is. Our ode to Trader Joe's!

Browse the recipes and try some too. If you have a favorite dish that you make with TJ items, upload your recipe and a picture or two. Love something you bought there? Or hate something you bought there? Let everyone know and submit a review.

Have fun and if you have any comments or suggestion, click below for contact information.

Thanks for stopping by! ~ Jenny and Shane Lees
